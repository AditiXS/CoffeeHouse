In the heart of Chandigarh, nestled among the cobblestone streets and whispered secrets of decades past, you’ll find Coffeehouse since 1972—a haven for coffee lovers, dreamers, and seekers of soulful moments. Our story began over four decades ago, when a passionate barista named Aditi decided to turn a cozy corner into something magical. Step into our cozy café and be greeted by the inviting aroma of freshly brewed espresso and the warm ambiance reminiscent of a traditional Parisian café. Our baristas are dedicated to crafting the perfect cup, whether it's a rich and velvety cappuccino, a bold espresso, or a smooth latte. Our kitchen serves up a delightful array of French-inspired dishes, from flaky croissants and buttery pain au chocolat to savory quiches and hearty baguette sandwiches. Each item is made with the finest ingredients, ensuring an authentic taste of France. Whether you're here for a quick coffee break, a leisurely brunch, कॉफीhouse provides the perfect setting.
